Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are your products safe for color-treated hair?
A: Yes! All our products are sulfate-free and color-safe.

Q: Are these cruelty-free?
A: Absolutely. We never test on animals.

Q:How do I apply the Hera Hair Oil?
A:Distribute a small amount in the palms of your hands first. Start applying from the tips of your hair and move upwards/towards your roots. For better distribution: flip your head upside down and gently scrunch or smooth the oil through your hair.

Q:Can I use all the products everyday?
A:Absolutely! All products are safe for daily use and they are designed to help maintain softness, protect from environmental damage, and improve long-term hair health.

Q:Are the products safe for color-treated or chemically processed hair?
A:Yes, the oil and heat protector are designed with natural ingredients and antioxidant-rich formula that support the health of color/chemical treated hair.
